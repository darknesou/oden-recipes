macarone with baschamel recipe

Hi fellas, today we will learn how to make a nice dish of macarone with baschamel! this dish is popular in the middle east in countries like Saudi arabia ,egypt etc

    ingredients:

  1. 375 grams of pasta
  2. 300 grams of boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  3. 1 large onion, sliced
  4. ½ teaspoon of salt
  5. ¼ teaspoon of black pepper powder
  6. 2 teaspoons of vegetable oil
  7. 100 grams of mozzarella cheese slices
  8. baschamelsauce

As for the baschamel sauce,you can either buy one or make it from scratch.

to make it from scratch you need :

  1. 5 cups of liquid milk
  2. 5 tablespoons of flour
  3. 300 grams of butter
  4. ¼ teaspoon of salt
  5. ¼ teaspoon of white pepper
  6. A pinch of black pepper powder
  7. butter

preparation method:

first Boil the pasta and the chicken, then drain them and place them in a baking dish. Sauté the onion and stir until it softens. Then season it with salt and black pepper, and add it to the chicken and pasta. now prepare the baschamelsauce,Melt the butter, then add the flour and whisk continuously until it turns golden brown. Gradually add the milk while stirring continuously until the mixture thickens. Season the mixture with white pepper, salt, and black pepper. Pour half of the cheese and sauce mixture into the baking dish, then mix the ingredients well and add the remaining cheese and sauce. Place the chicken pasta dish in a preheated oven at 190°C (375°F) and leave it for 30 to 40 minutes until it browns. serve and enjoy!
